Materials

If you prepare the materials ahead of the lesson, you may have more time to focus on teaching.
You may choose to format the material component of a lesson plan by creating bullet points for each material you and the students might need during the lesson.
Here are some materials typically used during a lesson that you can consider adding to your list:

Textbook
Pen or pencil
Lined paper
Printed copies of worksheets
Markers
Scissors
Calculators
Tablets Laptops
