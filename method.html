teaching method

summary

This course will introduce the basic concepts and applications of sports demonstration teaching method. This teaching method aims to help students learn new motor skills and strategies through demonstration and imitation

Teaching goals

  • Understand the principles and applications of sports demonstration teaching methods
  • Mastering how to design and implement a demonstration teaching plan
  • Understand how to evaluate students' learning outcomes
